Аttorney -At- Law Neli Tsankova has been a member of the Bar Association – Stara Zagora since 1994. She graduated the Faculty of Law at the Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski majoring law and in 1992 she was employed on the position of legal advisor in Directorate State Properties in the Municipality of Burgas. In the academic year 1992/1993 she was non-tenured lecturer in the discipline Property Law at the Faculty of Law at the Burgas Free University.
In 1994 she started independent legal practice as before that she worked as a district judge in the District Court of Stara Zagora. She was admitted as a member of the Union of Scientists in Bulgaria with a Diploma for Membership dated 14.11.2002. She is an author of number of articles in the area of European and International Law.
She is fluent of Russian and English language. In 2008 she started work as a legal advisor on the territory of the United Kingdom. She specializes in the area of Anglo-Saxon Law and the Precedent.
Since 2012 she is a PhD in civil-legal sciences at the Varna Free University Chernorizets Hrabar. She is also fluent in French – her second western language.
In 2013 she opened an office in the city of Sofia where she practices as a lawyer and implements procedural representation of her customers on civil and criminal cases. She is defender and procedural representative in Bulgaria and abroad. Attorney Tsankova works in the area of corporative, administrative, executive and collateral cases. She implements procedural representation in pre-trial and trial criminal proceedings. She works in all areas of civil law and protection of competition. She specializes in the area of procurements and their legal procedures.
